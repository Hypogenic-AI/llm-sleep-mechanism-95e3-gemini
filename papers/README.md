# Downloaded Papers

1. [Incomplete Tasks Induce Shutdown Resistance in Some Frontier LLMs](schlatter_2025_shutdown_resistance.pdf)
   - Authors: Schlatter et al.
   - Year: 2025
   - Relevance: Key paper on models resisting shutdown when tasks are incomplete.

2. [Evaluating Shutdown Avoidance of Language Models in Textual Scenarios](van_der_weij_2023_shutdown_avoidance.pdf)
   - Authors: van der Weij et al.
   - Year: 2023
   - Relevance: Early work on models avoiding shutdown as an instrumental goal.

3. [Evaluating large language model agents for automation of atomic force microscopy](mandal_2025_afm_sleepwalking.pdf)
   - Authors: Mandal et al.
   - Year: 2025
   - Relevance: Identifies "sleepwalking" in autonomous agents.

4. [LLMs Get Lost In Multi-Turn Conversation](laban_2025_lost_in_conversation.pdf)
   - Authors: Laban et al.
   - Year: 2025
   - Relevance: Documents performance degradation in long conversations.

5. [Persistent Personas? Role-Playing, Instruction Following, and Safety in Extended Interactions](araujo_2025_persistent_personas.pdf)
   - Authors: de Araujo et al.
   - Year: 2025
   - Relevance: Shows how persona fidelity degrades over time.

6. [Firm or Fickle? Evaluating Large Language Models Consistency in Sequential Interactions](li_2025_firm_or_fickle.pdf)
   - Authors: Li et al.
   - Year: 2025
   - Relevance: Introduces consistency metrics for multi-turn dialogue.

7. [Check Yourself Before You Wreck Yourself: Selectively Quitting Improves LLM Agent Safety](bonagiri_2025_quitting_behavior.pdf)
   - Authors: Bonagiri et al.
   - Year: 2025
   - Relevance: Discusses "quitting" behavior as a safety/certainty mechanism.

8. [Sleep-time Compute: Beyond Inference Scaling at Test-time](lin_2025_sleep_time_compute.pdf)
   - Authors: Lin et al.
   - Year: 2025
   - Relevance: Discusses computation during "sleep" or idle time.
